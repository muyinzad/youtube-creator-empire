# SECTION 1: AdSense Optimization

## Maximize Ad Revenue

### Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Is | How to Improve |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| RPM | Revenue per 1K views | Higher watch time |
| CPC | Cost per click | Better keywords |
| CTR | Click-through rate | Better thumbnails |
| Watch Time | Total hours | Longer, engaging content |

---

### RPM by Content Type

| Content Type | Typical RPM |
|--------------|-------------|
| Tech Reviews | $2-8 |
| Vlogs | $1-4 |
| Tutorials | $3-10 |
| Gaming | $1-3 |
| Educational | $3-15 |
| Entertainment | $1-4 |

---

### Ad Placement Strategy

**Best Places for Ads:**
- After intro (before content)
- Mid-roll (every 8-10 minutes)
- End screens (before outro)

**Avoid:**
- First 30 seconds (limits retention)
- Over mid-roll ads too frequently

---

### Optimize for RPM

1. **Increase watch time**
   - Hook in first 10 seconds
   - Remove boring parts
   - Add engaging elements

2. **Target high CPC keywords**
   - Finance, business, tech
   - Reviews with product names
   - How-to tutorials

3. **Create longer content**
   - 10-20 minute videos
   - More ad breaks = more revenue

# SECTION 4: Merchandise

## Selling Your Own Products

### Merch Platforms

| Platform | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| Teespring | Easy, low risk | Lower margin |
| Merch by Amazon | Amazon traffic | Approval needed |
| Spreadshop | Good margins | Less traffic |
| Custom | Full control | Upfront cost |

---

### Best-Selling Merch Ideas

1. **Channel Logo** shirts
2. **Catchphrases** / inside jokes
3. **Mascot** art
4. **Quotes** from videos
5. **Niche-specific** designs
6. **Reference** designs (memes)

---

### Merch Pricing Strategy

```
Product Cost: $10
+
Platform Fee: $3
+
Shipping: $5
=
Cost: $18

Retail Price: $28-35
Profit: $10-17 per sale
```

---

### Merch Launch Strategy

1. Announce in videos (2-3 mentions)
2. Link in description
3. Create dedicated video
4. Promote on social media
5. Offer discount for email list
