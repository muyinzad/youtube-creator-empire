# SECTION 5: Niche Selection Framework

## The Perfect Niche Checklist

### Must-Have (Check All)

- [ ] I'm genuinely interested in this
- [ ] I have expertise or can learn
- [ ] Audience exists and is searchable
- [ ] Can create 100+ videos on this
- [ ] Can be monetized
- [ ] Low enough competition to enter
- [ ] Not saturated

---

### Scoring Your Niche

Rate each 1-10:

| Factor | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Interest/Passion | /10 |

| Monetization | /10 |

**Below 50 = Keep looking**
