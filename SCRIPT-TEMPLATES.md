# SECTION 1: Tutorial Scripts

## Tutorial Script Formula

```
[HOOK - 0:00-0:30]
"Today I'll show you [BENEFIT] in [TIME]. By the end of this 
video, you'll [DESIRED OUTCOME]."

[INTRO - 0:30-1:00]
"Who are you? Why should they watch? What's the benefit?"

[MAIN CONTENT - 1:00-XX:XX]
Step 1: [First step]
Step 2: [Second step]
Step 3: [Third step]
... continue

[CONCLUSION - Last 2 minutes]
- Recap what they learned
- Key takeaways
- Call to action

[OUTRO - Last 30 seconds]
- Subscribe button
- Like button
- Watch another video
```
