## Thumbnail Ideas Bank

### For Tutorials
- Step numbers + text
- Before/after
- Arrow pointing to result
- Person with focused expression

### For Vlogs
- Big face + emotion
- Time of day
- Activity image
- Blur background

### For Reviews
- Product comparison
- Rating (stars)
- "Worth It" vs "Skip"
- Products laid out

### For Lists
- Numbers (1, 5, 10)
- Grid of items
- "Best of" badge
- Ranked items
